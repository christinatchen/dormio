\centered **AMENDMENT TO THE EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT BY AND BETWEEN**

\centered **[[Person 1 First Name]] [[Person 1 Last Name]], JR.,**

\centered **[[Organization 2 Name]] AND [[Organization 1 Name]]**

**WHEREAS,** [[Person 1 First Name]] [[Person 1 Last Name]], Jr. (the “Executive”) entered into an employment agreement with [[Organization 2 Name]] (the “Bank”) and [[Organization 1 Name]] (the “Company”) effective [[Date 1: Date]] (the “Employment Agreement”); and

**WHEREAS** , the Board of Directors of the Bank has resolved to amend the Employment Agreement effective [[Date 2: Date]], to reflect an adjustment in the Executive’s annual base salary; and

**WHEREAS**, the Employment Agreement was further amended to provide that modifications to Executive’s base salary in the future will not require an amendment to the Employment Agreement.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, the Bank, the Company and the Executive hereby consent to the following:

Effective [[Date 2]], Section 4(a) of the Employment Agreement shall be deleted in its entirety and replaced with the following new Section 4(a):

“The Bank agrees to pay the executive an annual salary of $[[USD Value 1: Number]] payable in accordance with the customary payroll practices of the Bank. The Executive’s annual salary, as the same may be modified from time to time, is referred to in this Agreement as the “base salary.””
